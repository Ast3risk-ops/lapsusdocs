## Tested distributions

<Info>Lapsus supports more distributions than this, this is just what it has been tested with.</Info>

- Ubuntu ≥18.04
- Debian ≥10
- FreeBSD 14
- Windows 10 & 11 (No WSL)
<Warning>
    Podman and Docker are not supported!
